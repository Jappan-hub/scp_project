Welcome to SCP Catalogue!

The SCP Foundation is a fictional organization featured in a collaborative writing project known as the SCP Wiki. Within this shared universe, the Foundation is portrayed as a secretive global entity dedicated to securing, containing, and protecting anomalous objects, entities, and phenomena—collectively referred to as "SCPs"—that defy natural laws. These anomalies range from living creatures and artifacts to locations and abstract concepts, each exhibiting unique and often dangerous properties.


SCP-002: The "Living" Room

SCP-002 is a highly dangerous, semi-organic entity capable of assimilating humans and transforming them into furniture. It remains under strict observation, as its true nature and origin are still unknown.


SCP-003: Biological Motherboard

SCP-003 is a biomechanical anomaly with the ability to self-evolve and adapt. Its potential intelligence and rapid growth capabilities make it both a scientific mystery and a containment risk.


SCP-004: The 12 Rusty Keys and the Door

SCP-004 is a potential gateway to unknown dimensions and represents a severe containment risk. Its true origins and the nature of the realms it leads to remain unexplored.


SCP-005: Skeleton Key

SCP-005 is a useful but highly sensitive asset that could pose a severe security threat if obtained by unauthorized personnel. It remains under strict containment to prevent misuse.


SCP-006: Fountain of Youth

SCP-006 represents one of the most valuable assets under Foundation control. While its potential could revolutionize medicine, its existence is kept secret to prevent global instability and potential conflicts over its use.
